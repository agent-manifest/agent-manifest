# Privacy

This page states what this site does, as a matter of fact. It is a description of
a static site, not a legal policy, and it makes no promises about anything other
than the pages served from `agent-manifest-spec.org`.

## What this site does not do

The site has no accounts, no sign-in, no payments, and no user-generated content.
It serves static documents only.

- It sets **no cookies** and reads none.
- It runs **no analytics and no tracking** of any kind.
- It contains **no forms**, so it receives no submitted data.
- It loads **no third-party scripts**, and stores nothing in your browser.

Nothing you do on this site is recorded by the project, because there is no
mechanism by which it could be.

## What third parties necessarily see

Two parties observe a request, because they serve it:

- **GitHub Pages** hosts and delivers these pages. Like any web host, it
  processes the request — including your IP address and user agent — under
  [GitHub's privacy statement](https://docs.github.com/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).
- **Google Fonts** serves the two typefaces this site uses, from
  `fonts.googleapis.com` and `fonts.gstatic.com`. Your browser requests those
  files directly, so Google receives your IP address, user agent, and the
  referring page. Google states that Fonts sets no cookies. This is the site's
  only third-party dependency, and it is a design decision recorded here rather
  than left implicit.

The project operates neither service and receives no data from either.

## If you write to us

Mail sent to the address on the [contact page](/contact/) is received and read by
the maintainer, and is kept only as long as needed to answer it. It is not added
to any list and is not shared.

## Machine-readable representations

The documents, schema, and citation metadata this site publishes are served as
static files. Requesting them involves no identification of the requester, human
or agent.
