# Contact

## Institutional contact

<contact@agent-manifest-spec.org>

This address reaches the maintainer of the Agent Manifest specification and of
this site. It is the project's contact path that does not require a GitHub
account.

## Who maintains this

Stewardship, the public surface principle, and how contributions are handled are

## What this channel is for

- Questions about the specification, its scope, or its terminology.
- Corrections to any published document or record.
- Citation, attribution, and archival matters.
- Notices of misattribution or of implementations that claim more than the
  specification states.

The project answers as time allows. It offers no service level, no support
contract, and no certification. Adoption of the specification is voluntary and
implies no endorsement by the maintainer.

## Security reports go elsewhere

**Do not use this address for vulnerabilities.** Security and safety concerns
must be reported privately, through the channel stated in the
[security policy](/SECURITY.html) and in
[`/.well-known/security.txt`](/.well-known/security.txt).

## Public channels

The source of record is the specification repository on GitHub. Issues and pull
requests are public and are the right place for defect reports and proposed
changes:
<https://github.com/agent-manifest/agent-manifest>.
